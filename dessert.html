Chewy Apple Oatmeal Cookies

Prep: 20 mins. Bake: 10 mins (batch + cooling)

My family loves oatmeal raisin cookies, but I wanted to try something new with the classic recipe.
We enjoy apples, and i thought the dried fruit would make a good cookie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 - 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups old-fashion oats
  • 1/2 cup chopped dried apples

Instructions

Preheat oven at 350 degrees.

  1. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugars.
    Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  2. Combine the flour, cinnamon, baking soda and salt; gradually add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Stir in oats and apples.
  3. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.Let stand for 1 minute before removing to wire rack to cool.

Yield: 4 dozen.
